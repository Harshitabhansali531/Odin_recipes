Classic Pancakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 and 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 and 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Steps:

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  2. Whisk the wet ingredients in another bowl.
  3. Combine and stir until smooth.
  4. Cook on a hot pan until golden brown.
